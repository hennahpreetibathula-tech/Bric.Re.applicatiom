# 🚀 Bric.Re – AI-Powered Research Assistant

## 📖 Overview

Bric.Re is an AI-powered research assistant designed to help users discover, analyze, and understand information through evidence-based insights.

The project was inspired by a common challenge faced by students and researchers: finding reliable information efficiently without spending hours searching through multiple sources.

To address this, Bric.Re explores Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) concepts to provide context-aware and research-focused responses.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Research often involves navigating numerous sources, articles, and documents to find relevant information.

While AI can generate answers quickly, ensuring that those answers are relevant, contextual, and useful remains a challenge.

Bric.Re aims to bridge this gap by combining information retrieval and AI-generated insights to create a more effective research experience.
